Manipulation & Information

Manipulation

1: Grasp handle with one hand to hold the rest of the umbrella above head


2: Use one finger or thumb to press button on the handle to extend umbrella


3: Use one hand to velcro/undo velcro around the collapsed umbrella to secure or release it


4: Place cord around one wrist for easy carrying when collapsed or to secure the umbrella when open


5: Use one thumb to press release switch to collapse the umbrella

History

Ancient Egypt: Palm leaves used to protect royalty from the sun


China: leather umbrellas are used only by the wealthy


Ancient Greece and Rome: Women use parasols to block the sun


Renaissance Europe: Wealthy women use umbrellas for shielding themselves from the sun


18th Century Europe: General public use umbrellas as accessories


1928: The first folding pocket umbrella is invented


Present Day: There are many different umbrella designs (ex: for high winds or dome/transparent umbrellas)
